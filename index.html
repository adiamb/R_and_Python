Aditya Ambati PhD

Professional Summary

Aditya Ambati is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Stanford University, Center for Sleep Sciences and Medicine. His research is focused on developing novel computational and experimental approaches to unravel the mechanisms and develop biomarkers in sleep and circadian disorders. He is a Proficient Python and R software developer with estabilished experience in executing biomedical, genetic and proteomic data science projects from concept to publication.
